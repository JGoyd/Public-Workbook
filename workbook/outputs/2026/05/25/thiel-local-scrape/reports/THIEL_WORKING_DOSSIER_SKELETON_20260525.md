## Core Thesis Draft

The corpus supports a three-lane dossier: Epstein/Thiel access, Valar capital solicitation and fund materials, and a Palantir/security-state context lane. The key layer matters because it elevated several high-value documents and contexts, especially the Valar fund packets and the September 2014 schedule lane, but the report should always cite the underlying source text.

## One-Line Frame Bank

- Thiel's Antichrist rhetoric sits beside a source trail of Epstein access, Valar capital asks, and Palantir state-power branding.
- The ICE hook is part of the machine, not the whole machine: the broader lane is Palantir as enforcement infrastructure.
- The cleanest corpus move is access to capital: Epstein proximity, direct Valar solicitation, and Valar packets selling Thiel/Palantir credibility.
