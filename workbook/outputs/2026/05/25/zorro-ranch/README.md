# 2026-05-25 · Zorro Ranch

A records-and-witness read on Zorro Ranch in New Mexico, focused on what
the source documents can actually test: who went, when they went, where
they stayed, who arranged it, and whether any New Mexico legal duty was
triggered.

Day: Monday
Topic: Zorro Ranch
Status: Subpoena-ready, not violation-proven

See also: [Thiel Reveal](../thiel-reveal/README.md) — overlapping 2015 New Mexico invite thread.

---

## Headline

Zorro Ranch is an actionable public-interest inquiry because the records show post-conviction guest access, staff-managed logistics, structured operational systems, ranch facilities, aviation access, telecom and security infrastructure, and a New Mexico registration question that was already legally salient in later litigation. The records support subpoenas and witness interviews. They do not yet prove a registration violation, records destruction, abuse at the ranch, or misconduct by named guests.

---

## What I found

Five evidence lanes, each with its own ceiling.

### 1. Post-conviction guest access was staff-managed

**Nicholas Jarecki, 2009 (Sept 27 – Oct 2 window)**
- EFTA02437328, EFTA00740016, EFTA00740019, EFTA00740021, EFTA02437641, EFTA02437618 — Jarecki asks if the ranch is "functional" and can host him; Epstein approves; Brice handles lodge/bunkhouse logistics; Warren and Linda are moved to the bunkhouse for the last day.

**Nicholas L. Ribis, 2010 (Oct 6 – Oct 8/9 window)**
- EFTA00732601, EFTA00732603, EFTA00755449, EFTA00896952, EFTA01811370, EFTA02419506 — Epstein offers Ribis the ranch with staff, horseback riding, guest lodge, and indoor-pool access.

**Peter Thiel, 2015 (Aug – early Sept window)**
- EFTA02490930, EFTA02492241, EFTA02357056, EFTA00652089 — Epstein invites Thiel; activities listed (ATVs, horseback riding, hiking); airport routing given (Albuquerque, Santa Fe, Moriarty — Moriarty "only 10 minutes from ranch"). _Cross-references the Thiel Reveal entry._

What this proves: staff-managed guest lodging post-2008.
What it does not prove: Epstein's own physical presence on those dates, or any guest's misconduct.

### 2. Zorro had structured operations and identifiable custodians

- **Contact / phone / log topology** — EFTA02040184, EFTA00334167.
- **Project & task lists** — EFTA00578213, EFTA00578182, EFTA00578197.
- **Staffing / property management** — EFTA00627582, EFTA00672499, EFTA01888044.
- **Maintenance / mechanical / facilities** — EFTA00586048, EFTA01132763, EFTA00586045, EFTA00586059, EFTA01111025, EFTA00675607.
- **Telecom / security / infrastructure** — EFTA01092673, EFTA01124805, EFTA02415807 (CenturyLink, T-1, microwave; Brice Gordon as main contact).

What this proves: defined record systems with identifiable custodians.
What it does not prove: misuse of those systems.

### 3. Aviation records are the most direct route to presence and duration

- EFTA00068582, DOJ-OGR-00011773 — Visoski testimony: ranch layout, runway, hangar, twin-engine Cessna 421, guest/pilot/chef lodging.
- DOJ-OGR-00013279 — pilot record categories: passenger manifests, aircraft logs, personal logbooks, departure/arrival columns, aircraft identifiers.
- EFTA01242527 — repeated Moriarty NM and Santa Fe NM phone entries (leads only).
- EFTA02490930, EFTA02357056 — Thiel-thread airport routing.

What this can do: prove or disprove Epstein's New Mexico arrival/departure and duration once FBO and FAA records are pulled.

### 4. New Mexico registration is the sharpest legal theory — but not proven

- EFTA00074029 — 2019 filing referencing an Aug 19, 2010 New Mexico Department of Public Safety letter stating Epstein was not required to register in New Mexico at that time for the 2008 Florida conviction.
- EFTA00180921, EFTA00184224 — broader registration / appellate context.

Status: investigation justified, violation not proven.

### 5. Preservation is a lead only

Record systems clearly existed (guest lodge line/fax/log, project/task lists, maintenance checklists with signature fields, telecom/security/HVAC, pilot manifests/logbooks). That supports preservation demands and retention-policy questions. It does not support a destruction or post-subpoena cleanup claim.
