## Executive Finding

Zorro Ranch is no longer just a property-background lane. The evidence supports an actionable New Mexico inquiry focused on post-conviction guest access, staff-managed lodging, ranch operations, aviation access, telecom/security systems, and records custodians.

The evidence does not yet prove a New Mexico registration violation, spoliation, or misconduct by named guests. It does support subpoenas and interviews because the records show identifiable systems, staff, facilities, vendors, and logs that could prove or disprove the missing thresholds.

## Core Theory

The strongest theory is not that any single guest email proves abuse. The stronger theory is that Zorro Ranch generated and depended on records that can be demanded:

- guest lodge / main house / bunkhouse lodging instructions;
- staff emails and guest travel details;
- Ranch Central office and phone/fax/log systems;
- gate, vehicle, HVAC, fire alarm, camera, and telecom systems;
- project/task lists and maintenance checklists;
- airport/FBO/pilot records and passenger manifests;
- NM DPS/county sex-offender registration correspondence.

That is enough to justify a Truth Commission or AG-style inquiry asking: who stayed, when, who arranged it, what records existed, who kept them, and whether Epstein's physical presence triggered any New Mexico legal duty.
