## Bottom line

`EFTA00609489` is the opening page of a 98-page confidential private placement memorandum for Valar Global Fund III LP (`EFTA00609489` through `EFTA00609586`). It does not, on indexed text, create a direct India/Ambani/Modi/Puri/Chopra/Mantha/Rafael/Barak link. The India language found in the PPM is private-placement notice language for prospective investors in India.

The high-value discovery is a separate financial lane: Southern Trust Company Inc. appears as a $25,000,000 limited partner / investor commitment to Valar Global Fund III LP, funded through a sequence of capital calls, Epstein approvals, wire instructions, Deutsche Bank statements, K-1 material, and Deutsche Bank transaction charts.

This lane matters because it gives a complete evidentiary model for how to harden a suspected Epstein-side financial transaction:

- commitment document or fund package;
- capital-call notice;
- internal approval email;
- signed wire authorization or bank instruction;
- bank-statement outflow;
- K-1 / partner capital account reconciliation;
- outside counsel / fund administrator / bank counterparties for subpoena targets.

It is not an India allegation by itself.
