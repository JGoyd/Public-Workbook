The Buenos Aires Bond Southern Financial LLC · Deutsche Bank · Argentina · 2018

Bottom line

In April 2018, Deutsche Bank structured an Argentina sovereign bond trade with an embedded EUR/USD cross-currency swap for Southern Financial LLC — trade ID G175714L, Summit code SOUTHFINANMD. Internal Deutsche Bank records state the client was "not adopted for Frankfurt location," constituting a KYC violation by DB's own policy language.

The trade settled via a SWIFT transfer of EUR 1,021,732.88 from DB Frankfurt to Southern Financial LLC account N4G023804, routed through Citibank Frankfurt and Pershing LLC. The Argentina bond was sold at a loss in May 2018; the swap was unwound with DB paying Southern Financial an additional EUR 30,000. No KYC closure document appears in the available record before the trade was completed.

Southern Financial LLC appears in the same Deutsche Bank private banking account cluster as the documented Epstein entity group — Jeepers Inc., The Haze Trust, Neptune LLC, Hyperion Air LLC — from at least 2013.

04 · KYC Breach

The Frankfurt adoption-location problem

Deutsche Bank's OTC derivative framework requires a client to be "adopted" — KYC-cleared — at the specific booking location where a trade is recorded. G175714L was booked against DB AG Frankfurt (FFT). Southern Financial LLC had not been adopted for Frankfurt; its existing adoption locations were London and/or the US booking center.
